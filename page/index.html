Dubai Integrated Economic Zones

Dubai Integrated Economic Zone (DIEZ) is a premier free zone organization formed in 2021 as a powerhouse free zone comprising the three leading free zones in Dubai, namely: Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZ), Dubai Silicon Oasis (DSO), and the Dubai CommercCity (DCC). It operates under the strategic direction of the Dubai Integrated Economic Zone (DIEZ), a fully owned subsidiary of the Investment Corporation of Dubai (ICD), enabling each existing free zone to operate independently as business units under DIEZ.
